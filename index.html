Billie Holiday

American jazz and swing music singer

Time line of Billie Holidays life:

  • 1915 - Billie Holiday Is Born April 7, Born In Philadelphia, Penn.
  • 1933 - Holiday Cuts Her First Records At The Age Of 18
  • 1938 - She Teams With Artie Shaw Becoming The First Black Woman To Work With A White Orchestra
  • 1948 - Performs At Carnegie Hall To A Sold Out Crowd
  • 1959 - Holiday gives her final performance in New York City. – July 17, Holiday dies in New York City and is buried at Saint Raymond’s Cemetery in Bronx County, NY.
